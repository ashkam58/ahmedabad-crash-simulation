Educational Simulation: Flight 113

This presentation is a simplified 3D reconstruction of the final moments of Indian Airlines Flight 113 on October 19, 1988. It is based on the official accident investigation report and is intended for educational purposes only, to illustrate the challenges of aviation in adverse weather conditions. This is a memorial to the lives lost, and we ask that you view it with respect and solemnity.
